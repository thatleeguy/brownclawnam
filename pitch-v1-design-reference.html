Why this firm exists

Equipment doesn't fail at random. It fails for reasons — and the reasons are usually upstream of the work order.

Most operations don't have a maintenance problem. They have a decision problem: too many priorities, not enough criticality, and a CMMS that records causes symptoms.

Brownclaw is built around one premise: reliability is engineered, not hoped for. We bring the methods — RCFA, FMEA, RCM, criticality, Weibull — and we bring them onto your floor, with the people doing the work, until the program is yours, not ours.

Connor Schriver, P.Eng, CMRP · Principal SIG / 2026.04 / FERNIE BC

Where to spend the next reliability dollar — and where not to.

Every site has a budget, and every budget has a fight. We rank equipment criticality across consequence (safety, environmental, production loss, repair cost) against probability (failure history, age, duty), and we hand back a defensible, signable register that survives audit and turnover.
